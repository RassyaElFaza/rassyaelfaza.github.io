About Me

Hello, I'm Rassya. I'm a 17-year-old artist and frontend developer. I love creating and experimenting with technology. I share my knowledge and skills on YouTube, where you can find tutorials and demos on frontend development and design. I also have experience in traditional and digital art.

My journey as an artist started at the age of five when I first picked up a pencil and started drawing. From then on, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career in the creative field. I spent my childhood experimenting with different mediums and honing my skills. In my teenage years, I discovered my interest in technology and the possibilities it holds for the future of art and design. That's when I started learning frontend development at the age of 16, and I've been exploring the intersection of technology and art ever since. It's been an exciting journey, and I can't wait to see what the future holds for me.

I'm dedicated to my craft, I hope you can follow my journey. Thank you for visiting my profile.
